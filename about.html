About FASHION HUB

A trusted, retailer-driven clothing marketplace built to connect verified African fashion sellers with buyers across the continent and beyond.

Our Mission

"To reduce fraud in online fashion retail by verifying every seller's identity, shop, and contact details — giving buyers confidence and giving honest retailers a platform they deserve."

FASHION HUB was built because informal fashion retail in East Africa is thriving but trust is a major barrier. Buyers don't know who they're buying from, and scammers exploit that gap. Our answer is a rigorous, multi-step retailer verification system — every single seller you see here has verified their national ID, phone, email, shop address, and business details.

How It Works

Two sides of the same marketplace — one for buyers browsing great fashion, one for retailers growing their business.

🛍️

For Buyers

  1. Browse the marketplace Filter by category (Men, Women, Kids), search by name, or sort by price.

  2. View product details Every listing includes a full description, available sizes, price, and the verified retailer's shop name.

  3. Add to cart & checkout Select your size, add items to your cart, and proceed to purchase with confidence.

🏪

For Retailers

  1. Register & get verified Complete our 3-step identity, shop, and document verification process.

  2. Verify your contacts Both your phone (OTP) and email must be confirmed before your account is active.

  3. Post products Once approved, use the seller dashboard to list clothes by category. Your shop name appears on every listing.

The Verification Process

Our 3-step verification ensures every listed seller is real and traceable.

🪪

Step 1 — Identity

Sellers submit their full name, National ID number, ID photo, selfie, phone number, email, and date of birth. Both phone (OTP) and email must be confirmed before moving forward.

🏬

Step 2 — Shop Info

Retailers provide their shop name, business type, county, town, physical address, an optional Google Maps link, and photos of the shop and signboard.

📄

Step 3 — Business Documents

Sellers can upload a Business Permit, KRA PIN document, and Business Registration Certificate. A declaration is required confirming all info is accurate.

✅

Approved Status

Only after all three steps are validated does the seller receive an approved status. Product posting is disabled for unverified accounts.
