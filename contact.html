Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a verified retailer?

Go to the Sell page and complete the 3-step verification.

Can I report a suspicious seller?

Yes — use the contact form and select "Report a Seller" as the subject.

Why is my verification not working?

Make sure both your phone OTP and email are verified before clicking Next on Step 1.

Is there a fee to list products?

No. Listing products is completely free for verified retailers.
